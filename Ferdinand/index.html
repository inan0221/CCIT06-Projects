Vexel Artworks

is a digital art that is an entirely pixel-based form of raster art that imitates the vector graphics technique, but is distinguished from normal vector graphics or raster images. “Vexel” itself is a mixture of two words 'Vector' and 'Pixel'. Vexel designs are composed by using multiple layered shapes.
